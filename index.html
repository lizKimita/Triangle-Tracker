Types of Triangles


Equilateral Triangle

For this triangle all the sides are equal.


Isosceles Triangle

In an Isosceles triangle, two sides are exactly equal


Scalene Triangle

When it comes to a scalene triangle, none of the sides is equal.

However, the sum of any two sides of the triangle must be greater than the third side.
